Orion brings a fresh approach to Professional Recruiting – with strong emphasis on the word “professional”.

We treat each recruiting opportunity as its own project. We develop a deep understanding of the job requirements and the type of personality that will be successful within the client’s direct team and the overall organization. At the same time, we constantly work to build relationships with candidates who represent the top people in their fields.

Our Clients

Our clients trust our process because we guarantee candidates will have the following attributes before we submit them for consideration. We do not waste our client’s time.
  • The candidate has the experience and skill set to do the job.
  • The candidate has the right personality to fit into the organization.
  • The candidate fits into the client’s compensation range.
  • The candidate is excited about the opportunity.

Candidate Pledge

Our candidates trust us because we act as a career partner and not just a recruiter trying to make a fee. Relationships are built on trust and respect, and we promise the following to our candidates:
  • We get to know your personal and career aspirations and will only present roles that help you achieve those aspirations.
  • We will fiercely protect your privacy. Your information will never be submitted to a client without your permission.
  • We will keep you informed of our progress throughout the recruiting process.
  • We will act as your advocate to ensure you get an offer that meets your personal needs.
